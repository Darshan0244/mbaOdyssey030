The Blindfold Challenge

RULES

1. This game will be played by 2 players, it consists of a minefield with a lot of obstacles (mines).

2. One player wears a blindfold and will be the one navigating the minefield.

3. The other player stands outside the minefield and gives instructions to the blindfolded player.

4. The blindfolded player starts at the designated starting point and must reach the finishing line safely without stepping on a mine.

5. The guiding player can give verbal instructions to help the blindfolded player move around, but cannot physically touch the blindfolded player.

6. If the blindfolded player steps on or touches a mine, they must return to the starting point and begin again.

7. The blindfolded player wins by reaching the finish line without touching any mines.

8. The time limit for the game is 2 minutes.
