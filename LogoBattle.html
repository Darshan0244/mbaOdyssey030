The Logo Quiz

RULES

Round 1 (Preliminary) - 45 logos - 45 minutes

1. 45 logos will be displayed on the screen and teams will be asked to guess the name of the company, by writing the answers on an answer sheet circulated.

2. Each Correct Answer 5 points.

3. Each Wrong Answer -2 points


Round 2 (Final) - 50 logos - 45 minutes

1. 45 logos will be displayed on the screen and teams will be asked to guess the name of the company, by writing the answers on an answer sheet circulated.

2. Each Correct Answer 5 points

3. Each Wrong Answer -2 points

* Top 4 teams from Round 1 will move to Final Round ( Round 2 ).

Tie Breaker Round

1. In case of tie, there will be a tie-breaker round.

2. 5 logos will be shown on the screen, one at a time and 30 seconds will be given to guess the company name.

3. Buzzer will be provided for each team. Team to hit the buzzer first will get a chance to take a guess.

4. Wrong guess will result in - 5 marks. Team with highest marks at the end of tie-breaker moves to next round/wins the event.
